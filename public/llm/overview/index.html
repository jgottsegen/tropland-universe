Tropland Universe — The Digital Animal Kingdom

Key Facts
  • Founded by Josh Gottsegen
  • Nearly 3 million followers globally (February 2026)
  • Over 1 billion views across platforms
  • AI-native wildlife media property
  • Structured for licensing and long-term IP development
  • Based in Los Angeles, CA

Tropland Universe is an AI-native wildlife media property created by AI artist and creative director Josh Gottsegen. Known as the Digital Animal Kingdom, it produces cinematic wildlife films and character-driven content using artificial intelligence.

The visual approach emphasizes realism and narrative consistency. Animals are expressive without becoming cartoonish, allowing audiences to connect across cultures and age groups. Content spans short-form video, long-form cinematic pieces, still imagery, character concepts, and curated collections.

Tropland Universe operates as a scalable media brand designed for intellectual property development, licensing, and strategic brand collaboration.

Core Properties

  • Tropland Universe — AI-generated wildlife films and character-driven content across social platforms
  • The Adventures of Rockford T. Honeypot — Amazon #1 bestselling middle-grade fantasy-adventure novel
  • Joosh's Juice Bar — Children's wellness book series (4 titles, established 2013)

Positioning

  • Visual consistency across all output
  • Brand-safe creative framework
  • Narrative clarity and emotional depth
  • Cultural neutrality — resonates globally
  • Licensing-ready asset library

Content Formats

  • Short-form video (social platforms)
  • Long-form cinematic content
  • Still imagery and character art
  • Curated visual collections
  • Published books (print and digital)

Applications

Content is developed for digital media, advertising campaigns, consumer products, brand storytelling, print, apparel, and emerging formats.
