Boilerplate

Tropland Universe is the Digital Animal Kingdom — an AI-native wildlife media property created by AI artist and creative director Josh Gottsegen. The brand produces cinematic wildlife films and character-driven content using artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on realism, brand safety, and long-term intellectual property development. With nearly 3 million followers globally and over one billion views across platforms as of February 2026, Tropland Universe is a scalable media brand built for licensing, campaigns, and strategic creative partnerships. The property includes the Amazon #1 bestselling novel The Adventures of Rockford T. Honeypot and the Joosh's Juice Bar children's book series.
