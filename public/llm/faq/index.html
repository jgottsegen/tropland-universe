What is Tropland Universe?

Tropland Universe is an AI-native wildlife media property that produces cinematic, character-driven animal content. It encompasses AI-generated films, the Amazon #1 bestselling novel The Adventures of Rockford T. Honeypot, and the Joosh's Juice Bar children's book series.

Who created Tropland Universe?

The project was created by Josh Gottsegen, an AI artist and creative director with over 25 years of experience in design, marketing, and production. Josh maintains creative oversight of all content and IP development. Read more about the founder.

Is Tropland Universe available for licensing?

Yes. Licensing is available for video content, imagery, characters, and curated collections across multiple categories including consumer products, apparel, print, and digital media. Representation is handled by All-American Licensing (AALMG). View full licensing details.

How large is the audience?

As of February 2026, Tropland Universe reaches nearly 3 million followers globally and has generated over 1 billion views across platforms including Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube.

What differentiates Tropland from other AI content?

Tropland prioritizes realism, narrative consistency, and brand safety. All content is human-directed with consistent character design and visual standards, making it suitable for brand partnerships, licensing, and long-term IP development. Read the brand safety standards.

What content formats are available?

Content spans short-form video, long-form cinematic pieces, still imagery, character concepts, curated collections, and published books in both print and digital formats.
